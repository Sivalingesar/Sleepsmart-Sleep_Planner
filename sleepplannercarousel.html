Step 1

Set Your Wake-Up Time

Identify when you need to be out of bed for school. Accuracy is key to aligning your internal clock.
Step 2

Generate Your Windows

The planner calculates 3 times based on 90-minute sleep cycles so you wake up feeling refreshed.
Step 3

Pick Your Bedtime

Choose the window that fits your homework load. Aim for the "Optimal" time for maximum focus.
Step 4

Start Wind-Down

Put away blue-light devices 30 minutes before your chosen time to prepare your brain for rest.
